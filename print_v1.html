Private Transfers

Day 1 - 25-July: Welcome to Switzerland

Smooth Arrival

Private van pick-up from Zurich Airport to Radisson Hotel & Suites Zurich.

Bahnhofstrasse & Promenade

Relax and stroll Zurich’s luxury shopping street; optional Lake Zurich walk.

Indian Dinner Options

Dine at Khana Khazana, Tamarind Hill, or New Bombay with convenient transfer.

Day 2 - 26-July: Zurich to Montreux

Lindt Home of Chocolate

Visit interactive chocolate museum & tasting session.

Glacier 3000 Cable Car

Ride up for Peak Walk & breathtaking glacier views.

Gstaad Village Stroll

Explore luxury chalets, cafes; stop at Saanen village for photos.

Day 3 - 27-July: Lakeside & City Highlights

Montreux Promenade

Morning lakeside walk & Freddie Mercury Statue photo stop.

Geneva City Tour

Visit Jet d’Eau Fountain, Flower Clock, Old Town, UN Headquarters.

Dinner Options

Return to Montreux for dinner or dine at top Indian restaurants in Geneva.

Day 4 - 28-July: Montreux to Zermatt

Scenic Drive

Drive to Tasch village and take the shuttle train or electric taxi to Zermatt.

Zermatt Village

Explore car-free Zermatt: Bahnhofstrasse street, Old Village, Matterhorn Museum.

Dinner Options

GoldenIndia Zermatt or Snowboat Café for Indian-style dishes.

Day 5 - 29-July: Zermatt to Matterhorn

Matterhorn Glacier Paradise

Ride Europe’s highest cable car, explore Glacier Palace and ice caves.

Optional Gornergrat Railway

Take the scenic train for panoramic Matterhorn views.

Zermatt Village

Evening stroll and shopping along Bahnhofstrasse.

Day 6 - 30-July: Glacier Express | Chur | St Moritz

Glacier Express

Board the world-famous panoramic train from Zermatt to Chur.

Vaduz Stopover

Short photo stop in Vaduz, Liechtenstein, with castle views.

St Moritz Arrival

Check in at Hotel Laudinella; optional Lake St Moritz walk.

Day 7 - 31-July: St Moritz | Italian Border Day Trip

Livigno, Italy

Optional day trip for duty-free shopping and beautiful alpine drive.

Bernina Pass

Explore scenic spots along Bernina Pass with panoramic views.

Muottas Muragl Funicular

Take the funicular for sunset over St Moritz and Engadin Valley.

Day 8 - 1-August: St Moritz → Interlaken

Scenic Transfer

Long but beautiful drive through alpine villages and valleys.

Sightseeing Stops

Pause for photos in charming mountain villages en route.

Interlaken Check-In

Arrive at Grand Hotel Beau Rivage; evening at leisure.

Day 9 - 2-August: Jungfrau & Harder Kulm

Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe

Train ride to Jungfraujoch via Kleine Scheidegg; visit Sphinx Observatory & Ice Palace.

Harder Kulm

Evening funicular to Harder Kulm for panoramic sunset views over Interlaken.

Dinner Options

Dine at local Indian restaurants back in Interlaken.

Day 10 - 3-August: Lauterbrunnen - Wengen

Staubbach Falls

Walk to Switzerland’s highest free-falling waterfall.

Trümmelbach Falls (Optional)

Explore unique waterfalls inside the mountain if open.

Wengen Village

Optional visit to the car-free alpine village by train or taxi.

Day 11 - 4-August: Interlaken → Lucerne (via Mt Titlis)

Mt Titlis

Ride the Rotair cable car, explore Glacier Cave, Ice Flyer & Cliff Walk.

Lucerne Arrival

Stroll Lake Lucerne Promenade, see Chapel Bridge illuminated at night.

Check-In & Dinner

Check in at Radisson Blu Lucerne; dinner at top Indian restaurants.

Day 12 - 5-August: Mt Pilatus & Lucerne

Mt Pilatus Cogwheel

Ride the world’s steepest cogwheel railway with panoramic mountain views.

Lake Lucerne Cruise

Optional lake cruise with stunning alpine backdrops.

Lucerne Old Town

Explore Chapel Bridge, Lion Monument, and old town squares.

Day 13 - 6-August: Departure from Switzerland

Check-Out & Transfer

Private van transfer from Lucerne to Zurich Airport.

Last-Minute Shopping

Optional stop for souvenirs before heading to the airport.

Fond Farewell

Goodbye from Vacation Vibes LLP – Travel Curators!
